## {{ description }}

Yes, I said I was getting back into this website stuff, and yes, it's been 3 months.

Mostly been busy getting outside, working and also doing nothing. Also executive function is hard to come by with me...

So inspired by some recent radio DXing events in my area, I have finally decided to hopefully sit down and slowly compile together my mess of random notes on my phone, discord chats and other stuff where I used to log my catches, into one place on this website!

Eventually I do also want to add airchecks (I already have some up on my file dump I might link to over in the Radio section of this site)

Perhaps some of those might eventually be TV recordings, but not very likely any time soon, I have much worse luck DXing TV stations considering how everything has moved to Digital, and how I don't really even get the best reception of semi-locals anyways.

Also was thinking of doing some sort of radio collection page down the line with pictures of all the radios I own, but only time will tell if I get around to that.

For now my main plan is to try and find as much of the old logs I've written as I can and compile information about those catches to put up here, mainly for my own reference but why not put it up online too!
